Frequently Asked Questions relating to your Blueant

How many devices can I pair to the Supertooth II?

The Supertooth can store pairing information for 2 different devices and connect to either when requested depending on which is in range and which was connected last. The Supertooth can only connect to one phone at a time. To connect to the second phone the first must be disconnected.

Is my phone compatible?

If your phone has Bluetooth it more than likely supports a Handsfree or Headset Bluetooth profile, and so will work with the Supertooth II. Check your phones manual or our compatibility list on the BlueAnt website for up to date information.

What is the passcode/passkey of the Supertooth II?

The Passcode for the Supertooth II is “0000”

Do I need to pair the devices every time I use them together?

No, the pairing process is required only once for each phone. If you pair more than two phones the first will be deleted and pairing will need to be done again.

Frequently Asked Questions relating to your iPod/MP3 Accessories

Question

What are some of the features of Logitech Wireless Headphones for iPod?

Answer

Q: What is Logitech Wireless Headphones for iPod?
A: Wireless Headphones for iPod is a wireless headphone that can be used with Apple iPod MP3 players.

Q: How long does the battery last once they are fully charged?
A: The battery life on a full charge can be up to eight hours. Both the headphones and the wireless receiver have to be charged.

Q: Which iPod models are compatible with Logitech Wireless Headphones for iPod?
A: Wireless Headphones for iPod is compatible with any Apple iPod with Stereo Minijack and Remote Connector plugs, including:
  • iPod Photo
  • iPod mini
  • iPod U2 Special Edition
  • 4th Generation iPod with Click Wheel
  • 3rd Generation iPod with touch wheel and buttons

Q: Which wireless technology is used in the Wireless Headphones for MP3?
A: Wireless Headphones for iPod uses Bluetooth® 1.2 wireless technology, which includes adaptive frequency hopping, for interference-free, crystal-clear digital audio.

Q: What controls are available on the headphone?
A: The + and - controls the volume level on the headphone. This is independent of the volume setting on iPod. The circular center button is multi-function. The multi-functions button can be power on/off as well as put the headphone into a discoverable mode to pair with the wireless receiver and pause. The ">" skips to the next song and the "<" goes back to the previous song.

Question

What are some of the features of Logitech Wireless Headphones for MP3?

Answer

Q: What is Logitech Wireless Headphones for MP3?
A: Wireless Headphones for MP3 is a wireless headphone that can be used with any MP3 digital audio players, CD players and other music devices with a standard 3.5mm stereo audio output.

Q: How long does the battery last once they are fully charged?
A: The battery life on a full charge can be up to eight hours. Both the headphones and the wireless receiver have to be charged.

Q: Does the Wireless Headphones for MP3 come in any other colors?
A: No, Wireless Headphones for MP3 is only available in black.

Q: Which wireless technology is used in the Wireless Headphones for MP3? A: Wireless Headphones for MP3 uses Bluetooth® 1.2 wireless technology, which includes adaptive frequency hopping, for interference-free, crystal-clear digital audio.

Q: What controls are available on the headphone?
A: The headphone controls the volume of the headphone. This is independent of the volume setting on the MP3 or the CD player. The circular center button is multi-function. The multi-functions button can be power on/off as well as put the headphone into a discoverable mode to pair with the wireless receiver.

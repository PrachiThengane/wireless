Warranty and Returns

  1. Damaged or DOA (Dead On Arrival) Items
  2. Faulty Items
  3. Refunds and Returns

  1. Damaged or DOA (Dead On Arrival) Items

    2. If the item you receive has been damaged in transit, please contact us within 1 business day of receiving the item and we will arrange an RA (Return Authorisation) Number so the item can be returned. We will replace the item once it has been returned to us.

    If your item is DOA (Dead On Arrival), please contact us within 1 business day of receiving the item and we will arrange a RA (Return Authorisation) Number so the item can be returned. DOA items are sent to the manufacturer or supplier for replacement. Once they have been replaced, the item will be forward to you. Unfortunately we can not offer on the spot replacements for DOA items.

    Please return Damaged or DOA items to us in original packaging with all contents and accessories as was supplied to you.

  2. Faulty Items

    3. Manufacturer or supplier warranty applies to all products sold by Wireless 1. Please consult your warranty card with your item for details on having your item repaired/replaced under warranty. If you require warranty information, you can also visit our Warranty Pages for all manufacturer contact details.

    If the manufacturer directs you to return the item to us, please contact us with your order number or invoice number and we will issue you with an RA (Return Authorisation) Number.

    Once your item has been received, it will be forwarded to the manufacturer or supplier under their warranty conditions. Unfortunately Wireless 1 has no control over warranty turnaround times, however we will endeavour to follow up your warranty item to ensure it is completed as fast as possible for you. Should you require any information regarding an item you have sent to us under warranty, please contact us with the RA number that we have supplied you.

    If returning a faulty item to us as directed by the manufacturer, please return it in the original packaging with all contents and accessories.

  3. Returns and Refunds

    4. We endeavour to have all DOA and faulty items replaced for you, however should you wish to have the item refunded, this is possible. Refunds are possible only when an item has been deemed faulty by the manufacturer/supplier and Wireless 1 has been credited for this. A refund can only be processed if all contents and accessories have also been returned with the item.

    Unfortunately we do not refund for incompatibility, so please choose carefully.

    Any item which is returned to us being DOA, Faulty or damaged is done so at the expense of the buyer.

  1. Can I change or cancel my order once it has been submitted?

    2. Please contact us within 2 hours of making payment to inform us of your intentions. Should you wish to cancel the item, there will be a refund charge of $2.50 payable.

  2. Do I get a Tax Invoice with my item?

    3. Yes our items are supplied with Tax Invoices. Please hold these for salary sacrifice and warranty purposes.

  3. How do I view my orders?

    4. To view your orders, please Login to your Wireless 1 account and click view orders.

  4. I want to pay for my item but it has been cancelled, how do I do this?

    5. If you have selected BPay as your payment option, and we have not received your payment within 7 working days, your order will be cancelled. This is done to be fair to other buyers who may wish to purchase the same item.

  5. Will I receive a confirmation of my order?

    6. Yes you will receive a confirmation or your purchase via email. We may also phone you to confirm your order and also request more information (Copy of driver's licence/Credit Card).

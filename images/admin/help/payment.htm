  1. What payment methods do you accept?

    2. For your convenience, we accept Mastercard, Visa, BPay and Paypal payments. These are processed through our Secure Comodo encrypted checkout.

  2. Do your prices include GST?

    3. Yes our prices are GST inclusive. No hidden extras!

  3. Why does it ask me for a PIN when using my Credit Card?

    4. At Wireless 1, we are committed to providing a secure environment to make your purchases safe and easy. We have implemented Mastercard Secure code and Verified by Visa which Mastercard and Visa have introduced to make purchasing products online, safer and secure for both the purchaser and the merchant. These PIN's act in the same way as they do for an ATM or handy card. They verify that the card belongs to you, and that you are making the purchase. These are important steps in ensuring that your credit card is not used for any unauthorised transactions.

    To find out more about Mastercard Secure code or Verified by Visa, please follow the links. Mastercard Secure code or Verified by Visa.

    You can still purchase from us should you not have a PIN for your card, however we may call you to confirm your order and ask for further information, such as a copy of your drivers' licence and credit card, to be faxed or emailed to us to ensure that the order is authorised by you as the card holder.

  4. Can I still purchase without a Credit Card?

    5. Yes you can. We also accept BPay and Paypal, so you can pay using your bank account.

  5. What is BPay?

    6. BPay enables you to pay for bills or accounts using your bank accounts or credit card. You pay directly through your financial institute using the invoice received from us. All you need to do is select BPay as your payment method, and you will receive your invoice with the Biller Code and Customer Reference Number for you to proceed with payment. To find out more about BPay, please visit the BPay website.

  6. What is Paypal?

    7. Paypal is a third party payment system which allows you to pay for your items using Credit Card or your Bank accounts without sharing any of your financial information. To find out more about Paypal, please visit the Paypal website.

  7. Can I change my payment method once submitted?

    8. Unfortunately once your payment has been submitted, we are unable to change the payment method.

  8. How long does it take to confirm my payment?

    9. Once you have made payment, please allow 24-48 hours for us to confirm the payment. Your item will generally be despatched within 1-2 working day of receiving payment.

Shop Securely with Wireless 1 Online

At Wireless 1 Online, we are committed to providing you with a safe and secure shopping experience. Our site and checkout facilities are encrypted and secured by Comodo SSL. This means that your information is protected and scrambled when in transit between different computers and networks.

For your security we do not store credit cards details or payment details on site or electronically as all payments are handled directly with our merchant bank account. This ensures the utmost security for your credit card and banking information.

Important Credit Card Information

At Wireless 1 we take fraud seriously, and as such, we take all necessary steps to ensure your purchase is secure. For larger orders we may request the following:

  • A faxed or email copy of your Drivers Licence and Credit card to ensure you are the credit card holder. The address on the drivers licence must match the delivery address.
  • Future orders after the first verification may be subject to random checks
  • All orders will be subject to name and address verification before being processed.
  • Any suspected fraudulent activity will be reported to the police.

    • We also follow the Privacy Act 1988 when it comes to handling your personal information, and you can visit our privacy policy for more information.

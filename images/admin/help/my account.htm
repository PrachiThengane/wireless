  4. How do I view my order?

  1. How do I login?

    2. Click the Login/Register link in the top right corner of the home page. If you have an account, enter your username and password.

    If you do not have an account, you can register with is by following the register link. Registering only takes a few minutes and gives you access to great prices as well as keeping you up to date on the latest products.

  2. I have forgotten my password, what do I do?

    3. At the Login screen, click the 'Forgotten Password' link. You will be asked to enter your email address. You will be emailed a temporary password to your email.

  3. How do I change details within my account?

    4. First you will need to Login as per the login instructions above. Once you have logged in, click 'My Account'. Here you will be able to edit details within your account.

  4. How do I view my order?

    5. After you have logged into your account, you can click the 'View Orders' link within your account. You will also be able to see whether the item has been despatched here.

  5. How do I register for an account?

    6. You can register by clicking the Login/Register link. It only takes a few minutes and once completed, you will have access to all our great deals.

The Samsung P580 boasts Genuine Windows® 7 Professional Edition and an Intel® Core™ i5 processor, providing you with performance and productivity to power through your workload. Add an ergonomic, durable design that can easily withstand all the traveling you do, and an innovative free-fall sensor that protects data in case of drops and falls by automatically parking its hard drive when movement is detected.

 

Because a life on the go can often trip you up, the Samsung P580 was crafted to not let you down. In fact, its high quality craftsmanship extends as much to its exterior as to its high performance inner workings. Built to withstand bumps and knocks, the P580 comes with a robust, durable casing designed to keep its contents safe and sound as well as reduce the risk of scratches and fingerprints. And its ergonomic, stylish design makes it as coolly eye-catching as it is reliable.
Accidents happen. And when they do, you’ll be worry-free. Because the Samsung P580 features an innovative hard disk drive free fall sensor* which immediately detects movement. So if you drop your notebook, the hard disk drive will automatically park and your data will be saved. Feel free to be worry-free — you’re protected by Samsung innovation. * The sensor can withstand approximately 300g of shock. If the HDD is parked, it can withstand 900g, but typing will pause for a moment.

Intel® Turbo Boost Technology intelligently and automatically allocates processing power where it’s needed most, so you have all the speed and performance you need to match your workload. And with the fast, optimised response times that its Hyper-Threading Technology delivers, you get more done with less wait. Give your to-do list the boost it needs with the powerful Intel® Core™ i5 processor.

iPad 2

Now with iOS 5 and iCloud, it just got even harder to put down.

There’s never been anything like iPad 2. It’s incredibly thin and light, yet has a superfast dual-core A5 chip and a battery that lasts 10 hours. It comes with built-in apps for things like email, browsing the web and taking and viewing photos, and there are over 140,000 more apps just for iPad available on the App Store. Over 200 new software features in iOS 5 let you do more than ever. And now iPad 2 works with iCloud, which stores your content and wirelessly pushes it to all your devices.

AppleCare Protection Plan

Every iPad comes with one year of hardware repair coverage and 90 days of telephone technical support. The AppleCare Protection Plan for iPad extends your coverage to two years from the original purchase date of your iPad. With the AppleCare Protection Plan for iPad, Apple experts can help troubleshoot issues with your iPad, including using iOS, connecting to wireless networks, using iCloud, and questions about iWork for iPad or other Apple-branded apps. You also get up to two years of iPad repair coverage, including the battery. Should your iPad need to be serviced under the plan, Apple technical support representatives can even set up a repair during the same call.

Accessories and Related Products

iPad Smart Cover

The iPad Smart Cover was designed alongside iPad 2 to be its perfect match: a thin, durable cover that magnetically aligns for a perfect fit. It automatically wakes your iPad when you open it. And it folds into a handy stand for reading, watching and typing.

iPad 2 Dock

Sync or charge your iPad 2 just by setting it in this dock. The audio line out port lets you connect to powered speakers via an audio cable (sold separately). It supports iPad accessories such as the Apple Digital AV Adapter and the iPad Camera Connection Kit.

iPad Camera Connection Kit

Import photos and videos from a digital camera with your camera’s USB cable or directly from an SD card. iPad supports standard photo formats, including JPEG and RAW. So photos can appear on your iPad moments after you take them.
Apple Digital AV Adapter

You can debut slides, movies, photos and everything that fills your iPad on an even bigger screen with the Digital AV Adapter. The 30-pin to HDMI cable connects your iPad to an HDTV, video projection screen or other HDMI-compatible display.

 Apple TV

With Apple TV, you can rent from the largest selection of HD movies and even watch live sports. Apple TV also lets you wirelessly stream movies, photos, music and more from your iPad 2 to your HDTV using AirPlay.

 iTunes Gift Cards

iTunes Gift Cards are perfect for anyone who enjoys one-stop entertainment. Each card is redeemable for music, movies, TV shows, apps, games, books and more in the iTunes Store, the App Store or the iBookstore.

BELKIN BASIC N150 MODEM/ROUTER

Belkin’s Basic N150 Wireless Modem Router is perfect for setting up a wireless network that has the range to easily cover an apartment or small home.

The Belkin Basic Modem Router features:
  • N technology for speeds of up to 150Mbps
  • Continuous signal for uninterrupted wireless for multiple users across all your applications
  • Security settings are preset to get you up and running safely

 

Easy setup

Get your secure wireless network up and running in minutes. Just three easy steps, and you’re up and running out of the box and onto the Web.

Security

WPS/WPA2 encryption standards and preset security settings get you up and running safely. One-touch Wi-Fi Protected Setup (WPS) securely connects multiple computers and devices with the push of a button.

The network that takes care of itself.

The SELF-HEALING App automatically detects and resolves network problems and runs routine maintenance scans to give you the clearest wireless channel. If attention is required, a notification will appear right on your screen—no more blinking lights under your desk.

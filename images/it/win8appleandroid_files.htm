Windows 8

Windows 8 platform was built with touch screen in mind. It is the same platform across mobile phones, tablets and PC.

Windows 8 removes the start button, although you have the choice of switching between the two. It uses tiles instead where you can organise your favorite applications and apps.

As windows 8 was designed with touch in mind, when buying a PC, you should strongly consider getting on with a touch screen. You can get around with a mouse, but I think its much easier to tap or slide with your fingers on the screen.

The tiles display real time information though them like weather and current exchange rates.

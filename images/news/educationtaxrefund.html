What can you claim for?

  • Laptops
  • IT peripherals (Keyboards, Anitvirus, Microsoft Office, USB Flash Drives)
  • Computer repairs

Who is eligible?

$750 for each eligible child in primary school - that is, a refund of up to $375

Example:

Asus Netbook $649
Microsoft Office Student $149
Logitech Mouse $29
Total purchase $827
Total eligible refund of max amount $750 of $827 = $375

$1,500 for each eligible child in secondary school - that is, a refund of up to $750.

Example:

Acer Aspire Notebook $1099
Norton Antivirus $79
USB flash drive $25
Total Purchase $1203
Total eligible refund is $1203 (Under the $1500 limit) = $601.50 refund.

How do I claim?

If you do not need to lodge a tax return, you may fill in the form on July 1 and mail to the tax office to get your refund.

If you are lodging your own tax return, just fill in the ETR section at item T6

If you are lodging your tax return via an accountant, just provide them with your Tax Invoice and ask them to claim on your behalf.

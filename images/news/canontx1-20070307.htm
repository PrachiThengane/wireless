Canon PowerShot TX1 Digital Camera

A stylish compact camera that also shoots HD movie. The PowerShot TX1 is simply ingenious.

Experience the best of both worlds with this camera combining the style of an IXUS with the functionality of a PowerShot. The PowerShot TX1 has an astounding 10x optical zoom with an Optical Image Stabilizer in an ultra compact body. This stylish TX1 offers creative functionality with HD motion movie shooting. The ergonomic design enables razor sharp, shake free long distance still and video shooting.

Some of the features include:

  • 7.1 Megapixel.
  • Canon 10x optical zoom lens with Optical Image Stabilizer.
  • High definition movie with stereo sound.
  • Super fast DiG!C III Imaging Processor with ISO 1600.
  • Face Detect with Auto Focus, Auto Exposure, Flash Exposure and in camera red-eye correction
  • Shoot HD Movie up to 4GB

The sleek and stylish design of the Samsung F480 has a huge 2.8 inch, beautiful colour touch screen with 240 x 320 pixel high resolution, making this handset a pleasure to look at.

When comparing the F480 to it’s big brother Omnia, you will find that it’s more perfect in size to be used as a mobile phone and to slot into your pocket or handbag. A 5MP camera features turns this stunning fashion conscious phone into a highly capable camera phone with autofocus and power LED flash which allows you to take clear shots even at night.

Music fans will always be entertained with the advanced music features, supporting multiple music formats, the embedded MP3 Player lets you download music files quickly through fast connection speeds

 

 

An expandable memory card slot allows you to add a MicroSD to suit your memory needs, no worries of running out of memory again.

With HSDPA speeds of up to 7.2Mbps you will now be able to enjoy the luxury of making video calls, surf the web or download documents faster and with more ease and convenience than ever before. The full browser also means that you can view websites in the same format as a PC.

The SAMSUNG F480 has all your multimedia requirements without compromising on size. Available in two colours: silver and pink. Hurry to be one of the latest.

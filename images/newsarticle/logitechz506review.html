If you've never truly experienced true surround sound, be it in games, movies, or even music DVDs then you'll be in for a treat with Logitech's new Z506 surround sound speakers. The Z506 speakers have a futuristic design in their elliptical shape which will complement most bedroom or office computer setups. Each of the 5 satellite speakers have both a driver and a tweeter built in, which is a slight change to the previous X-530 and X-540 models of the last few years.

Setting up the speakers is a very quick and simple process, with all of the cables colour coded. From then on it's just a matter of deciding where you want them placed. The 75 watts RMS power that the Z506 speakers produce is more than enough to fill a large room, and you will be able to hear every sound from every angle in your games and 5.1 channel audio movies.

The Z506 speakers from Logitech provide awesome bang for your buck sound and you'll be overwhelmed with the sound quality they produce.

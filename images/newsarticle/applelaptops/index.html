Purchasing an Apple laptop

Are you ever confused on which Apple laptops to buy? So many good looking, sleek designs and don't know which one to pick? Or which Apple laptop is more suitable for you?

This review will guide you to the selection of your needs.

Do you travel? Or are you ever at home? Don't you just hate it carrying heavy laptops while going to work, library or even the cafe? Then the MacBook Air is for you.
At just 11inch and 13inch, this fast, thin and light Apple laptop is designed perfectly to fit your needs. Simply browse, chat and create presentations as this Apple laptop lasts up to 5hrs for the 11inch, 7hrs for the 13inch and up to 30days of stand-by time!

Are you a designer? Do you work with heavy duty files that slows or freezes your screen? Then the Apple laptop you are looking for is the MacBook Pro.
With the minimun of Intel Core i5 and its RAM of at least 4GB, you will have no problem regardless how many programs are opened.

Other awesome features.
Apple laptop provides you the Multi-Touch trackpad, giving you more than just a scroll up and down! Apple laptops are the first to let you tap, pinch, swipe, drag, rotate and scroll with ease. Apple laptop also provides you the High Definition when you Factime with your mates and your family.
Thunderbolt, the fastest, most versatile I/O ever in Apple laptop history. Thunderbolt connects the next generation of high-performance peripherals to the next generation of computers, having more than 12 times faster than FireWire 800 and up to 20 times faster than USB 2.0, and it offers unprecedented expansion capabilities.

History of Apple Laptops

As a brand of Macintosh, built by Apple, the first Apple Laptop was released in 2006. Starting with Intel Core Duo, 512MB of RAM and 80GB of Hard Drive, Apple Laptops have rapidly grown to become the most controversial notebooks today with its sleek and unique design.
